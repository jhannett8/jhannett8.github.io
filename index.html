About

I'm an experienced software developer passionate about coding.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a degree in biomedical sciences and a minor in chemistry from SUNY Cortland in May of 2017. During my time as an undergrad, I participated in a three-year-long independent research study involving the therapeutic potential of adipose derived stem cells (of which, has been published in 2018), volunteered at the local hospital, established clubs on campus, participated in multiple honors societies, and even tutored complex areas of study; these include Calculus, Physics, Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, and Human Physiology.

After acquiring some experience in different facets of the biomedical industry, I decided to enhance my skill-set in software development by enrolling in The Tech Academy's 'Full Stack Software Developer' Program. This intensive, 8 month program provides in-depth training in essential languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, C# and more.

I'm currently working under the umbrella of multiple companies, acquiring a wide array of employable skillsets. Amongst these include WordPress/Front-end Development, React Native Mobile Development, and Salesforce Engineering, as well as Search Engine Optimization.
