About

Joseph Hannett is a software developer passionate about coding.

In May of 2017, Joseph had graduated Summa Cum Laude with a degree in biomedical sciences and a minor in chemsitry from SUNY Cortland. During his time as an undergrad, Joseph participated in a three-year-long independent research study involving the therapeutic potential of adipose derived stem cells (of which, has recently been published in early 2018), volunteered at the local hospital, established clubs on campus, participated in mulitple honors societies, and even tutored his fellow students in complex areas of study; these include Calculus, Physics, Organic Chemsitry, Inorganic Chemistry, and Human Physiology.

After obtaining experience in different facets of the biomedical industry, Joseph decided to change career paths and follow his passion for software development. He is currently enhancing his software development skillset through The Tech Academy's 'Full Stack Software Developer Bootcamp.' This intense, 6 month program has provided Joseph with training and experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, C# and more. By the end of the bootcamp Joseph will be an experienced full-stack developer and would love to work with you on your project. Contact him below!
